BLUE MORPHO

Submitted by user iosnborn22. Blue morpho butterflys wings are bright blue, edged with black. Blue morpho is among the largest butterflies in the world, with wings spanning from five to eight inches. When blue morpho flies, flies, the contrasting bright blue and dull brown colors flash, making it look like the morpho is appearing and disappearing.
